Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick resolutions to common queries regarding trial registrations, ages, and scholarship allocations.

What is the minimum age profile required for cadet trials?

We accept athletes starting at age 6 for our fundamental motor skills and basic co-ordination classes. Competitive tournament and elite pathway divisions are structured for cadets aged 14 to 21.

How are student admissions and scholarship trials organized?

Trials run quarterly at our Arena District complex. Candidates are evaluated on structural velocity, joint coordination, strategic decision-making, and discipline. Top percentile candidates are recommended for scholarship adjustments.

Does the academy provide school tutoring or education balance?

Yes. Our elite pathway cadet program integrates study hours, academic counseling, and tutoring modules directly alongside athletic training schedules to maintain optimal school performance.

Can external scout networks attend academy league matches?

Yes. Registered scout agencies can obtain season schedules, game video reels, and player diagnostic booklets from our athletic records office. Reach out via email to register your credentials.
